# Power
Rate at which energy is transferred
# Formula
$$P = \frac{W}{\triangle t}=\frac{\triangle E}{\triangle t}$$
$$P = F(\overrightarrow v)$$
### SI Unit
$Watts(W)$
# Power Rating
- Higher power for a blow-dryer, the faster it blows your hair.
- Hair dryers tend to operate at 1000W
